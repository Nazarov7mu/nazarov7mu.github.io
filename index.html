Games

Currently we are focusing on games for Android, but have plans to extend platforms to iOS and PC.

Neon Hero

Neon Hero is a cyberpunk platform shooter with nice cyberpunk-inspired graphics, visual effects and cool retrowave music tracks.
We took inspiration from 1980s films, video games, magenta neon, and gridlines and mixed everything with the futuristic environment, neon, geometric figures and sick synthwave soundtracks.

Dodgers

This is “Dodgers”, colourful and challenging runner arcade game! With great graphics and exciting music, you won’t be able to stop once you start. Try out different styled maps with figures, emojis, memes(Wide Walking Meme, Coffin Dance Meme), noir and troll faces. New songs of different genres on each map will keep you entertained!
